About Sikor Consultant

We're a team focused on AI product development and IT consulting. We partner with developers and teams to ship better systems—no heavy sales, no rigid playbooks. We focus on delivery and long-term partnership.

Mission & values

We believe technology should enable business outcomes without unnecessary complexity. Our values shape how we work with every client.

  • Delivery first — We commit to scope and milestones and communicate clearly when things change.
  • Partnership — We act as an extension of your team. You get direct access to the people doing the work.
  • Pragmatism — We choose the right tool for the job and avoid over-engineering or vendor lock-in where it matters.
  • Integrity — We're transparent about what we can and can't do. We'd rather say no than over-promise.
